Name
Facsimile
/fakˈsɪm.ɪ.li/ — noun
An exact copy of a document, print, or other item. From Latin fac simile — "make similar." The tool creates a facsimile of any web page as editable Figma layers.

Design principles

Warm, not sterile

Beige canvas is the foundation. Everything sits on warm paper, not clinical white.

Quiet until it's not

Mostly neutral. Amber and dark ink are the only loud moments — use them with intention.

Serif for soul, sans for service

The italic serif gives Facsimile its personality. Sans body copy stays functional.

The name does the work

"Facsimile" is the entire identity. No icon, no symbol. The word is distinctive enough.
